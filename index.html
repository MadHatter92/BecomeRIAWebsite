What is a SEBI Registered RIA/RA?

Registered Investment Advisors (RIAs) are licensed professionals who provide personalized financial advice to individuals. They must follow strict fiduciary standards, meaning they are legally obligated to put their clients' interests first.

Research Analysts (RAs) are individuals or firms that publish reports or make recommendations on stocks, mutual funds, or other securities. They are required to maintain objectivity and disclose any potential conflicts of interest.
